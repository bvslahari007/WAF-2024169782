TechFest 2026

An Annual Technical & Cultural Fest of GITAM University

Date: 15th - 17th September 2026

About the Event

TechFest 2026 is our college's biggest technical and cultural festival that brings together students from various colleges to showcase their talent, creativity, and technical skills. The fest includes competitions, workshops, guest lectures, and cultural performances.

Highlights of the fest include:

  • Hackathons and coding competitions
  • Robotics and project exhibitions
  • Cultural dance and music performances
  • Guest lectures by industry experts
